# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, caste, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, political belief, race, religion, sex, sexual orientation, or socioeconomic status.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Communicate clearly and professionally
- Give credit where credit is due

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments related to protected characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Violent or abusive language
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior. They will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Reporting Guidelines

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this code of conduct, please report it by:
- Contacting the project maintainers directly
- Opening a confidential issue
- Emailing the team lead

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All project team members are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

## Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior

- Verbal warning
- Temporary suspension from participation
- Permanent expulsion from the community
